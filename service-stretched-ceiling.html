Stretched Ceiling Installations in Malta

Stretched ceilings are a premium ceiling solution using a thin PVC membrane tensioned onto an aluminium perimeter profile. The result is a perfectly flat, seamless ceiling surface that can be matte, gloss, satin or even printed with a custom image. They can also create a "sky lighting" backlit effect when combined with LED panels behind the translucent film.

Stretched ceilings are fast to install, require no drying time, and can be combined with gypsum perimeters for designer finishes.

What Our Stretched Ceiling Service Includes

  • Aluminium profile installation around the room perimeter
  • PVC membrane tensioning and heat-forming
  • Matte, gloss, satin and translucent membrane options
  • Integrated downlight modules cut into the membrane
  • LED backlit "sky effect" panels
  • Custom printed membrane (photos, artwork, patterns)
  • Combined gypsum perimeter and central stretch ceiling
  • Acoustic membrane options for noise reduction

Materials We Use

  • High-quality PVC stretch membrane (Barrisol, Clairia or equivalent)
  • Extruded aluminium perimeter harpoon profiles
  • LED panel modules for backlit effects
  • Integrated spotlight rings compatible with membrane
  • Acoustic PVC fabric for sound-absorbing ceilings

Why It Matters

Stretched ceilings eliminate the need for painting and hide uneven structural ceilings completely. They are especially useful in apartments where the concrete soffit is not level, saving significant time and cost compared to traditional floating and painting methods.
