Property Maintenance in Malta

Owning a property requires ongoing care to protect its value and keep it safe and comfortable. At Lazarus Turnkey Contractor, we offer a reliable property maintenance service to handle all the small and medium-sized jobs that come up over time — without the hassle of finding multiple separate tradesmen.

Whether you need a leaking pipe fixed, a damaged tile replaced, a fresh coat of paint, or a circuit that has tripped and won't reset, our experienced team handles it efficiently, cleanly, and professionally.

What Our Maintenance Service Includes

  • Electrical fault finding and circuit repairs
  • Socket, switch and lighting fixture replacement
  • Leaking tap, valve and pipe repairs
  • Toilet, basin and shower fitting repairs
  • Cracked or loose tile replacement
  • Re-grouting of tiled surfaces
  • Interior and exterior painting touch-ups
  • Plastering patch repairs and crack filling
  • Waterproofing membrane repairs on terraces and roofs
  • Furniture assembly and fixture mounting
  • Door and window adjustments
  • General property inspections and snag-list completions

Who We Work With

Our maintenance service is available to homeowners, landlords, property managers, and businesses. We work on apartments, villas, townhouses, commercial offices, and retail spaces across all of Malta.

We are also happy to support property management companies requiring a reliable subcontractor for ongoing maintenance contracts.

Why Choose Us for Maintenance?

Unlike calling separate electricians, plumbers, and painters, we offer a single point of contact for multiple trades. Our team coordinates internally, which means faster resolution, less disruption, and consistent quality across all works carried out.
